About.

KNOW was founded by four brothers on a mission to revolutionize local news. Staying informed in your community is challenging—finding stories that are both reliable and relevant takes time and effort. KNOW makes it simple. We use a machine learning algorithm to identify the top stories, which are then written by real people. Our news is algorithmically curated, but human-reported.

Join us in delivering fast, reliable, and impactful local news—day in and day out.
